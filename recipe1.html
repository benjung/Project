Recipe #1: Kimchi Fried Rice with Pork Belly

Step 1: Heat the oil in a wok or a large pan over medium high heat. Once almost smoking, add the onions and fry until translucent. Add the garlic and fry until they soften.
Step 2: Push the onions and garlic aside and sear the pork on all sides.
Step 3: Once the pork is almost cooked, turn the heat to high. Add the kimchi, kimchi juice and soy sauce. Stir to combine. The pork will start to absorb the flavor.
Step 4: Once the pork is cooked through, add the rice, chili paste and sugar. Fry to combine. This fried rice (unlike other fried rice) is meant to be on the moist side.
Step 5: Taste and add salt according to your preference. Swirl in the sesame oil and give it one last stir.
Step 6: Serve with delicious sunny side eggs.
